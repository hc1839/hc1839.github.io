Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename D = long>
class tanuki::interop::ForeignIterator< T, D >

Iterator over decorated opaque items of constant size.

Template Parameters
TType of decorated opaque items being iterated over. It must
  • have a constructor, T(void *), that wraps the given pointer to the opaque item,
  • meet the requirements of CopyConstructible without copying the opaque item,
  • meet the requirements of Destructible without deleting the opaque item,
  • not take ownership of the pointer, and
  • maintain the same pointer throughout its lifetime.
Dstd::iterator_traits::difference_type.

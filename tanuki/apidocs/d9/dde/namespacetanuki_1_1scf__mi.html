Parameters
mpi_commMPI communicator.
proj_factorsProjection factors. It must not be empty.
sys_h_opSystem Hamiltonian operator in bra-ket matrix. Its size must agree with the factors in proj_factors.
unit_basis_firstBeginning of the range of ket matrices of basis for each unit.
d_eff_h_mat_firstBeginning of the destination range of effective Hamiltonian matrices in in the representation of unit basis set for each unit.
d_mo_energies_firstBeginning of the destination range of ascending energies of spatial molecular orbitals for each unit.

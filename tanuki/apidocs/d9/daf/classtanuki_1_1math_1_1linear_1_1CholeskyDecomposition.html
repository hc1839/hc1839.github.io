Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class tanuki::math::linear::CholeskyDecomposition< T >

Cholesky decomposition of a matrix.

Decomposition is lazily performed when the lower triangular matrix or its conjugate transpose is requested.

Template Parameters
TType of elements in an Armadillo matrix. It must be tanuki::number::real_t or tanuki::number::complex_t.
